About

Who I Am

A graphic designer based in Gading Serpong, working across VFX, Motion, and 3D.

My background started in a different field entirely, but design pulled me in early and never let go. I care about work that is both good-looking and genuinely functional — clean systems that hold up under real use, not just a nice first frame.

Outside of client work I keep a running practice of small type and motion studies, mostly as an excuse to keep learning new tools.
